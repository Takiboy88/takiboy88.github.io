# Privacy Policy for Plagiarism Detector

**Effective Date:** November 2, 2025  
**Last Updated:** November 2, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to Plagiarism Detector ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Text Content
- **Analysis Data**: When you use our plagiarism detection feature, we temporarily process the text you submit (original text and text to check). This data is:
  - Stored locally on your device
  - Used only to perform plagiarism analysis
  - Saved in your analysis history for your reference
  - Never transmitted to our servers or third parties
  - Can be deleted at any time through the app's history feature

### 2. Usage Analytics
- **Analysis History**: We store analysis results locally on your device, including:
  - Original text submitted
  - Similarity scores
  - Detected plagiarized sections
  - Analysis summaries
  - Timestamps of analyses

### 3. Advertising Data
Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process:
- Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
- IP address
- Device information (model, OS version)
- Ad interaction data
- General location data

For more information about how Google processes your data, please review:
- [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. **Core Functionality**
   - To perform plagiarism detection and analysis
   - To save and display your analysis history
   - To generate analysis reports and summaries

2. **App Improvement**
   - To understand how users interact with the app
   - To improve our plagiarism detection algorithms
   - To fix bugs and optimize performance

3. **Advertising**
   - To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
   - To measure ad performance and prevent fraud

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All text content and analysis results are stored locally on your device using Android's Room database
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
- Data is encrypted at rest using Android's built-in encryption mechanisms

### No Server Transmission
- Your submitted text content is NEVER sent to our servers or any third-party services
- All plagiarism analysis is performed locally on your device
- We do not maintain any central database of user-submitted content

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### 2. Advertising Choices
- **Opt-Out of Personalized Ads**: You can opt out of personalized advertising by:
  - Visiting [Google's Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/)

- Analysis results can be exported as PDF or text files through the app's export feature
- Exported files can be shared or transferred as needed
